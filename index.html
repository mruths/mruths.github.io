The festival of lights called Diwali is Archie’s favorite holiday. She loves that it reminds people about light and hope and strength.

This year, Archie has invited some school friends to her family’s Diwali party. She’s excited and worried at the same time— what if they don’t like the holiday as much as she does?

When a thunderstorm drenches the decorations and knocks out the electricity, Archie is sure the holiday is ruined. How can there be a Festival of Lights without any lights?
