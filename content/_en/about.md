short_bio:
  - <b>Mitali Banerjee Ruths</b> grew up in Houston, Texas. And today, she lives in Montreal, Canada with her husband, three kids, and their dog, Tux.<br/>
  - When Mitali was younger, she dreamed of becoming an astronaut. Mitali has NOT gone into space, but she has done a lot of other interesting jobs! She has worked as a babysitter, an ice cream seller, and even a medical doctor. Now, she's an author!<br/>
  - Mitali has written several popular and award-winning children's books, with more coming out soon! She's the author of Archie Celebrates Diwali and the follow-up picture book Archie Celebrates an Indian Wedding.<br/>
  - Mitali also writes <a href="/en/books-pd">The Party Diaries</a>, the Scholastic Branches early chapter book series, named one of the Best Books of 2023 by the New York Public Library and the Chicago Public Library. Kirkus Reviews called the first book in the series, Awesome Orange Birthday, "a layered, upbeat tale starring a determined, relatable protagonist."<br/>
