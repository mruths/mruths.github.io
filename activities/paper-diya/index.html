Make a paper diya

What is a diya?
On Diwali, people around the world light millions of diyas. Traditionally they're little oil lamps made out of clay, but you can make your own diyas with paper and a few simple supplies.
What You Need
  • Paper
  • Pencil
  • Circle shape to trace (ex. cup, bowl, inside a roll of tape)
  • Scissors
  • Glue
